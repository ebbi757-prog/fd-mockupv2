About Our Firm

Our Story

A Legacy Built on
Trust & Results

Fairchild & Dalrymple was founded in 2013 with a commitment to providing high-quality legal representation to individuals and businesses throughout Central Virginia. What began as a focused boutique practice has grown into a multi-service law firm capable of handling the full spectrum of legal matters our clients face.

Our firm is a subsidiary of Fairchild & Yoder PLLC, which extends our reach and expertise across a broader range of legal disciplines. This affiliation allows us to offer seamless, comprehensive representation while maintaining the personal attention that defines our practice.

We represent businesses from $1,000 to multi-million dollar revenues and are proud to go to bat for every one of them. Whether you are facing a criminal charge, navigating a complex business transaction, or planning for your family's future, we bring the same dedication to every matter.
